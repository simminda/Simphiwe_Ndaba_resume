About Me

Simphiwe Ndaba is a a professional accountant with over 10 years of experience in handling comprehensive accounting tasks. Whether you run a small business or a growing enterprise, I specialize in managing payroll processing, debtors and creditors reconciliations, financial statement preparation, and month/year-end close processes. My goal is to simplify your financial management, ensuring you stay compliant while maximizing efficiency.

I have expertise in using various accounting software, including Sage, Xero, QuickBooks, Pastel, and Accpac. Whether you need help with reconciling accounts, generating accurate reports, or setting up an accounting system from scratch, I’ll deliver high-quality results. I’ve also successfully managed cash flows, processed month-end journals, and performed financial audits across industries like logistics, consulting, and maintenance.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for numbers, I’m here to help you achieve peace of mind by handling all your accounting needs professionally and promptly.
